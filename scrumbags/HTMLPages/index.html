Welcome to the SCRUMBAGS TEAM WEBSITE!

The four of us come from different backgrounds, and we each have different interests and goals. But one way or another our individual journeys led all of us here – to Boise State University's Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile technology program. Our hope is that this site will help visitors get to know each of us a little better.

We like to think of the GIMM program not just as a collection of shared classes, but as one great big family...

Jonathan Gaige

Jonathan (you can call him Joe) is currently attending Boise State University for a bachelor’s degree in Games and Interactive Mobile Media. He hopes to become a front end app developer with this degree. He has extensive knowledge in a range of Adobe programs, Unity based programs, and in Maya. In his ongoing classes he is learning more to code in C#, PHP, HTML, and Swift for IOS. He is learning and continuing to develop his skills for game building, animation, website creation and much more. His personal hobbies are gaming, fishing, rock hunting, camping, hiking, tennis, and playing around with applications and projects that he is currently working on.
